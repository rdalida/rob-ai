About School-Based Medicaid Services

School-Based Medicaid Services (SBMS) provide critical health and related services to eligible students, helping schools deliver comprehensive support while receiving Medicaid reimbursement.

Impact

Millions of students receive services through school Medicaid programs annually.

Coverage

Programs vary by state with different eligibility and service requirements.

Funding

Federal matching funds support states in providing essential student services.
